# EVENT ORGANIZATION GUIDE
## Food for the Capital (FFTC)

---

## OVERVIEW

FFTC follows a staged preparation timeline for every event. Lead organizers begin
planning up to 8 weeks before the event date. This structure ensures stores,
volunteers, and food banks are all coordinated well in advance.

---

## PREPARATION TIMELINE

### 8 Weeks Before
- Create a Google Drive folder for the event, named by month and year.
- Duplicate and update the volunteer registration form.
- Identify target store locations on a spreadsheet.

### 6 Weeks Before
- Begin store outreach.
- Divide target stores among outreach team members.
- Assign a consistent FFTC point of contact per store for continuity.

### 4 Weeks Before
- Contact partner food banks.
- Notify them of the confirmed event date.
- Confirm availability of donation boxes and posters.
- Arrange post-event donation pickup logistics.

### 3 Weeks Before
- Recruit volunteers via Instagram and the FFTC mailing list.
- Organize sign-ups by store location.

### 2 Weeks Before
- Post promotional content on social media and other channels.

### 1 Week Before
- Confirm volunteer availability for each location.

### 1 Day Before
- Send reminder emails to confirmed volunteers and store contacts.

---

## KEY OPERATIONAL TAKEAWAYS

- FFTC plans events with significant lead time — stores are contacted 6 weeks out.
- Every store has a dedicated FFTC point of contact from first call through event day.
- Follow-up by email and reminder calls are standard practice close to the event.
- Volunteer coordination is organized by location to prevent scheduling conflicts.
- Food bank logistics (boxes, posters, pickup) are confirmed at the 4-week mark.
