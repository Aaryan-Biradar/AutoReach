Lead organizers follow a staged preparation timeline for FFTC events:

8 weeks before:
- create a Google Drive folder for the event using the month and year
- duplicate and update the volunteer registration form
- identify target stores on a spreadsheet

6 weeks before:
- begin store outreach
- divide stores among outreach team members
- try to keep the same FFTC member as the main point of contact for each store

4 weeks before:
- contact food banks
- notify them of the event date
- ask about boxes, posters, and pickup after the event
- arrange how the donations will be collected afterward

3 weeks before:
- recruit volunteers through Instagram and the mailing list
- keep volunteer signups organized by location

2 weeks before:
- post promotional content

1 week before:
- confirm volunteer availability

1 day before:
- send reminder emails

Operational takeaway for the voice agent:
- FFTC plans events ahead of time
- outreach is structured
- the team can follow up by email and coordinate reminders closer to the event
