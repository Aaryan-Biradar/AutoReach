## AGENT NOTES
- Use the overall totals (19,378 lbs and $20,087) when citing cumulative impact.
- Use the "close to 20,000 lbs and $20,000" figure only when following the call script
  (that figure is intentionally conservative for verbal pitches).
- The awards list reinforces credibility if a store questions FFTC's legitimacy.
- Always tie impact back to local Ottawa food banks — stores respond well to
  knowing donations stay in their community.
