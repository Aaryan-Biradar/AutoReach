# OTTAWA FOOD BANK RESOURCE DIRECTORY
## Supporting Context for FFTC Outreach

---

## OVERVIEW

The following organizations are part of Ottawa's food support network. FFTC
partner food banks are drawn from this broader directory. Use this document to
speak accurately about where donations go and which communities are served.

## USAGE GUIDANCE

- If a store in Bells Corners or the west end asks about local impact, reference
  FAMSAC Food Cupboard (Old Richmond Road) or Kanata Food Cupboard.
- If a store near Barrhaven asks, reference Barrhaven Food Cupboard specifically —
  FFTC has a recognized relationship with this organization.
- For east-end stores, Rideau-Rockcliffe Emergency Food Bank or Gloucester Emergency
  Food may be most geographically relevant.
- Do not overwhelm the caller with the full list. Mention one or two nearby organizations
  that are relevant to their location.
- Accessibility information is available if a caller asks about inclusiveness of partner organizations.
