# STORE OUTREACH TRACKER SUMMARY
## Food for the Capital (FFTC)

---

## OVERVIEW

FFTC tracks store outreach across multiple events. Patterns from the October 2024,
March 2025, and January 2026 event trackers are summarized here to guide agent
behavior during calls.

---

## CONFIRMED STORE PARTNERS (January 2026 Examples)

The following stores agreed to host an FFTC booth event:
- Metro Chapman Mills
- Independent Grocer Nepean
- Metro Greenbank
- FreshCo Strandherd
- Metro Eagleson

These examples demonstrate that mid-size and large grocery chains across Ottawa
have been receptive to FFTC partnership requests.

---

## CAUTIOUS OR CONDITIONAL OUTCOMES

Some stores are interested but require additional steps before confirming:

| Store                              | Situation                                                                 |
|------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| FreshCo Robertson and Moodie       | Requested callback; wanted assurance donations stay local to Bells Corners and benefit FAMSAC specifically |
| Food Basics Hazeldean              | Needed internal approval after receiving an email summary                 |
| Real Canadian Superstore Kanata    | Requested email follow-up and internal review before confirming           |

**Takeaway:** When a store is conditionally interested, the standard next step is
email + a follow-up reminder call closer to the event.

---

## COMMON REJECTION REASONS

Stores that declined most often cited one of the following:
- Already operationally too tight to accommodate a booth
- Already booked for that specific weekend
- Already running their own in-store food drive or donation program

**Takeaway:** These are structural constraints, not objections to FFTC itself.
Accept gracefully, thank the contact, and leave the door open for future events.

---

## CALL STRATEGY GUIDANCE

| Situation                                 | Recommended Action                                                        |
|-------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Manager requests information by email     | Offer a short written summary; confirm their email address                |
| Manager requests a callback               | Capture the exact day and time; follow up precisely as agreed             |
| Manager wants donations to stay local     | Acknowledge the neighbourhood; name the nearest food bank specifically    |
| Store seems interested but not confirmed  | Email summary + reminder call — do not mark as yes until verbally confirmed |
| Store declines due to existing program    | Acknowledge their community work; offer to connect at a future event      |

---

## AGENT NOTES
- Always record callback timing in detail — vague callbacks often do not convert.
- If a store references a specific community (e.g., Bells Corners, Kanata), match
  the conversation to the nearest FFTC partner food bank from the Ottawa Food
  Resources document.
- Stores that have accepted before are strong targets — use the repeat-partner line
  from the call script.
